Software Development Experience


21 Months of Full-Time Experience?

I completed three full-time software development interships in the past three years as a part of earning my undergraduate degree ammounting in twenty-one months of experience (These internships are called co-ops at Northeastern University). The first co-op was with Goldman Sachs and the second and third co-ops were with an inbound marketing automation company called HubSpot. The details of these co-ops are described below. I learned as much if not more on these co-ops as I do than when I am in classes, and the experience has helped me to focus my studies and reaffirmed that I am certainly in the right field.

Each co-op presented different challenges, both technically and professionally and I have no doubt that the experience I’ve gained will be invaluable as I seek full-time employment for July 2017. Once I gain enough experience I hope to be able to leverage the management half of my degree and leadership experience and become a Tech Lead.

HubSpot

June 2016 - Dec 2016

June 2016 – December 2016 (7 months)Cambridge, Massachusetts At HubSpot, I worked on the Blog Publishing team maintaining and building the blog portion of HubSpot's product. I utilized frameworks like Dropwizard, Guice, Jackson, Mockito, and JUnit to build maintainable and powerful RESTful services building on my experience with these frameworks from my experience with my first co-op with HubSpot.

In August, I started building HubSpot's new Accelerated Mobile Pages product offering. The message to customers was that by simply turning AMP on, all of their blog posts, old and new would be published as google AMP content. I wrote about 80% of the code for the product in conjunction with a tech lead and senior engineer that both worked remotely from different cities. The product was announced at Inbound 16 and customer reactions are viewable here: https://twitter.com/search?q=%23inbound16%20amp&src=typd&lang=en

I also maintained and added a variety of changes to the Blog Team's Front End React and Flux applications.
